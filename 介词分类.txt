介词按词义分类


1)	表示地点（包括动向），如 about, above, across, after, along, among, around, at, before, behind, below, beneath, beside, between, beyond, by, down, from, in, into, near, off, on, over, through, throughout, to, towards, under, up, upon, with, within, without 等。

[注]有不少表地点的介词可表动向,除很明显的 across, around, over, towards, near 外，还有 among, behind, beneath, between, on, to, under 等。

2)	表示时间，如 about, after, around, as, at, before, behind, between, by, during, for, from, in, into, of, on, over, past, since, through, throughout, till(until), to, towards, within 等。

3)	表示除去，如 besides, but, except 等。

4)	表示比较，如 as, like, above, over 等。

5)	表示反对、赞成，如 against, for, with 等。

6)	表示原因、目的，如 for, with, from 等。

7)	表示结果，如 to, with, without 等。

8)	表示手段、方式，如 by, in, with 等。

9)	表示所属，如 of, with 等。

10)	表示条件，如 on, without, considering 等。

11)	表示让步，如 despite, in spite of, notwithstanding 等。

12)	表示关于，如 about, concerning, regarding, with regard to, as for, as to 等。

13)	表示对于，如 to, for, over, at, with 等。

14)	表示根据，如 on, according to 等。

15)	其他，如 without(没有) 等。




介词按结构分类


(一) 单词介词(single-word preposition)

封闭性的词类, 不能再派生新词。单音节介词不带重音。

1. 原始介词(Simple p.)

古代就有的。共有20 个: after, at, by, down, for, from, in, near, of, off, on, over , past, since, through, till, to, under, up, with

注: 从拉丁语、法语等来的介词, 在国际交际中(如: 体育、法律、科技)偶尔用到, 但并没有完全“ 转变适应”(acclimatized) 。
如: vs. = versus 比、对抗; via 通过; qua 作为; re 关于; vis-a-vis 相对

2. 复合介词(Compoun...)

由两个单词合成或加词缀构成:

(1) 带前缀a-的: aboard, about, above, across, admidst, against, along, among, around

(2) 带前缀be-的: before, behind, below, beneath, beside, besides, between, beyond

(3) 带前缀in-的: inside, into

(4) 其他: onto, outside, until, underneath, unlike, toward(s), upon, without...


3. 分词介词(Participle p.)

由分词转化来的多音节词一般都带重音。

(1) 由 -ing 分词转化来的: barring (除⋯⋯之外) , concerning(关于) , considering (考虑到) , during (在⋯⋯ 期间) , excepting(除⋯⋯之外) , including (包括) , notwithstanding (虽然) , pending(到⋯⋯为止) , regarding (关于) , respecting (关于) , saving (除⋯⋯之外) , touching(关于)

(2) 由 -d -t -n 分词转化来的: except (除⋯⋯ 之外) , past (通过) , given(考虑到) , granted(假定⋯⋯, 就算⋯⋯)

(二) 多词介词(Multi-word preposition)

开放性的词类, 可以产生新词。重音在末尾介词前面的词上。

1. 二词介词(Two-word prepositions)

(1) ...for 型: as for 至于, but for 若没有, except for 除了

(2) ...from 型: apart from 离开, away from 不在; from behind 从后面, from under 从下面, from across 从对面

(3) ...of 型: ahead of 在⋯⋯前面, because of 因为, instead of 而不, out of 从, regardless of 不管

(4) ...to 型: according to 根据, as to 关于, due to 由于, near to 近乎, next to 几乎, on to 到⋯⋯上, owing to 由于, prior to 在⋯⋯之前, up to 直到⋯⋯

(5) ...with 型: along with 和⋯⋯ 一道, together with 和⋯⋯一起

2. 三词介词(Thr ee-word prepositions)

(1) in...of 型: in aid of 作⋯⋯之用, in behalf of 代表, in case of 以防万一, in charge of 负责, in consequence of 由于, in face of 面对, in favo(u)r of 支持, in front of 在⋯⋯ 前面, in light of 依据, in lieu of 代替, in need of 需要, in place of 代替, in quest of 为了寻求, in respect of 在⋯⋯方面, in search of 查实, in spite of 尽管, in terms of 用⋯⋯术语, in view of 由看来

(2) in...with 型: in accordance with 与⋯⋯一致, in common with 与⋯⋯相同, in comparison with 与⋯⋯比较起来, in compliance
with 依从, in conformity with 依照, in connection with 与⋯⋯ 相连, in contact with 和⋯⋯联络, in line with 和⋯⋯一致

(3) by...of 型: by dint of 基于/由于/靠, by means of 通过, 按照, by virtue of 由于/根据, by way of 经过, by reason of 凭⋯⋯理由

(4) on...of 型: on account of 由于/因为, on behalf of 以⋯⋯名义/代表, on top of 在⋯⋯顶上, on pain of 违者处以⋯⋯

(5) in...for 型: in exchange for 交换, in return for 作为报答

(6) in...to 型: in addition to 加之, in relation to 与⋯⋯有关, in fairness to 为了对⋯⋯公平合理

(7) in /with...to 型: with /in regard to 关于, with /in reference to 关于, with /in respect to 关于

(8) as...as 型: as far as 至, as long as 只要, as soon as 一⋯⋯就⋯⋯, as late as 迟至, as much as 几乎等于, as many as 多达

3. 四词介词(Four-word preposition)

(1) 0 度型带 at 的: at the center of 在⋯⋯的中心, at the top of 在⋯⋯顶上, at the cost of 以⋯⋯为代价, at the back of 在⋯⋯后面,
at the foot of 在⋯⋯脚下, at the mercy of 由⋯⋯ 支配, at the bottom of 在⋯⋯底上, at the hands of 在⋯⋯ 手里, at the expense of 由⋯⋯
负担, at the front of 在⋯⋯前头
 
(2) 1-2 度型带 on 的: on the top of 在⋯⋯顶上, on the right of 在⋯⋯的右边, on the left of 在⋯⋯ 的左侧, on the east of 在⋯⋯的
东边, on the strength of 受⋯⋯ 的鼓励, on the ground of 基于⋯⋯理由

(3) 2-3 度型带 in 的: in the middle of 在⋯⋯中部, in the midst of 在⋯⋯ 中间, in the case of 就⋯⋯ 来说, in the front of 在⋯⋯ 前
部, in the bottom of 在⋯⋯底上, in the way of 阻碍, 挡路, in the room of 代替, in the eyes of 从⋯⋯ 眼光看来, in the hands of 在⋯⋯ 掌握之中, in the days of 在⋯⋯时代, in the name of 以⋯⋯的名义

(4) 带 under 的: under the direction of 在⋯⋯ 的指导下, under the control of 在⋯⋯控制下, under the charge of 在⋯⋯管理下, under
the command of 在⋯⋯指挥下, under the guidance of 在⋯⋯引导下, under the care of 在⋯⋯ 关照下, under the protection of 在⋯⋯ 庇护
下, under the influnce of 在⋯⋯影响下

(5) 带 by 的: by the side of 在⋯⋯ 的旁边, by the hand...of 经⋯⋯之手

(6) 带 with 的: with the exception of 除⋯⋯ 之外, with a view to 以⋯⋯为目的

(7) 带 as 的: as the result of 由⋯⋯ 的结果, as a means of 作为⋯⋯的方法

(8) 带 for 的: for the sake of 为了⋯⋯ 起见, for the purpose of 为⋯⋯目的
